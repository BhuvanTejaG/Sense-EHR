About Project

We developed an application, which notifies patient and doctor about the patient's health details. The patient carries a wearable sensor, so the data collected and processed is being sent to doctor for recommending medication and monitor patient's health. A recommendation on fitness settings and medication is provided based on previously collected Electronic Health Records (EHR) if there are any deviations from clinical quality metrics (CQM's).
